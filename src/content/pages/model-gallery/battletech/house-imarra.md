## **WARRIOR HOUSE IMARRA CAPELLAN CONFEDERATION c. 3063**

**Competed 05/19/23  
Catalysts Miniatures & 3d Prints  
Paints used:**  
**Pro Acryl:** Yellow Green, Dark Yellow Green, Golden Yellow  
**Vallejo:** White Ink, Metal Color Jet Exhaust

[Warrior House Imarra](https://www.sarna.net/wiki/Warrior_House_Imarra) is one of the Capellan Confederation's Warrior Houses, small but elite forces with a fanatical devotion to the Confederation and Chancellor. These are some of the few units formed after the First Succession War but before the technological changes starting in 3028. This makes the Warrior Houses interesting, beyond their nature as elite fanatical forces.

Laio have some very cool mech designs from the 3060s and as Catalyst has been slow to get to them we've got some 3d prints in this force. The bases are also 3d printed and I really like them, very easy to paint urban basing.

For the mechs I have leaned on this being an elite force with access to cutting edge mechs. These are balanced out by a few of the confederation's workhorses and I really like the mix. I think the paint scheme for Imarra is striking and works great to convey a Liao force green as an accent rather than primary color.

I plan to expand them with some Fa Shih Battle Armour, a common Liao mark Imarra would use extensively. I also want to paint up some vehicles for them from a Confederation Reserve Cavalry unit.

**HEAVY BATTLE LANCE:** CTF-3L Cataphract; Y-H9G Yu Huang; STK-5M Stalker; LHU-2B Lao Hu

**MEDIUM BATTLE LANCE:** RFL-6X Rifleman; VND-4L Vindicator; HBK-5N Hunchback; LHU-2B Lao Hu

**RECON LANCE:** RVN-4L Raven; SYU-2B Sha Yu; CDA-3F Cicada; PXH-4L Phoenix Hawk
