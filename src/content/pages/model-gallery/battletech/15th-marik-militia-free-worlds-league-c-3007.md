## **15TH MARIK MILITIA FREE WORLDS LEAGUE c. 3007**

**Competed 4/26/2025  
Catalysts Miniatures  
Paints used:**  
**Pro Acryl:** Royal Purple, Plum, Coal Black, Dark Silver, Orange Red, Brown  
**Two Thin Coats:** Runic Purple; Marine Blue; ELysium Blue; Evil Eye Red; Emperor Red; Scabbard Brown, Vambrace Brown; Talisman Green; Craven Yellow; Trooper White  
**Oils:** Abteilung 502 - Engine Grease; art store Violet

The [15th Marik Militia](https://www.sarna.net/wiki/15th_Marik_Militia) is a Battletech unit. I decided to paint it up for the connection to Snord's Irregulars, another force I'm working on. The unit is a pretty typical Free Worlds League unit during the 4th Succession War when the conflict between the two units takes place. As Free Worlds League militia didn't have dedicated vehicle formations I've supplemented them with an armored company from the local planetary militia.

The Fire Lance is taken from the 1986 supplement [Cranston Snord's Irregulars](https://www.sarna.net/wiki/Cranston_Snord%27s_Irregulars_(scenario_pack)) and while not a technically legal Fire Lance for Alpha Strike I'm going to say it counts.   
  
From 3052 the 15th was heavily involved with the Word of Blake so I'm considering expanding in that direction in the future.

**RECON LANCE:** LCT-1V Locust; WSP-1A Wasp; STG-3R Stinger; FLE-17 Flea

**FIRE LANCE:**  
WVR-6M Wolverine; CRD-3R Crusader; ARC-2R Archer; MAD-3M Marauder

**BATTLE LANCE:**  
RFL-3N Rifleman; UM-R60 Urbanmech; BJ-1 Blackjack; OTL-4D Ostel

**ROCHELLE PLANETARY DEFENSE COMPANY:**  
Galleon Light Tank Lance; Vedette Medium Tank Lance; Ontos Heavy Tank Lance
