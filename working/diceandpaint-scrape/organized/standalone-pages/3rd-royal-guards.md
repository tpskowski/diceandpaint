## **3RD ROYAL GUARDS LYRAN COMMONWEALTH** c.3060

**Competed 05/08/23  
Catalyst Miniatures  
Paints used: ?**

[The 3rd Royal Guards](https://www.sarna.net/wiki/3rd_Royal_Guards) are among the best equipped, best trained, and most loyal Lyran units. I wanted to do them for one simple reason, their parade paint scheme rules. Battletech offers very few chances to paint units with a lot of metallics, and even fewer with gold.

Beyond the paint scheme it's fun to do a Lyran unit because they skew very heavy, honestly I could have done more assaults here. I went with a reinforced company and included some later mech designs so that I can use this in earlier time periods as a standard strength company. As the 3rd is an RTC I have plans to add a company of tanks and some infantry in the future.

Fighting Pirannha Graphics doesn't make transfers for the 3rd so they have the Royal Guards organizational level logo.

**COMMAND LANCE:** AS7-D Atlas; AWS-8Q Awesome; ZEU-6S Zeus; WHM-6R Warhammer

**ASSAULT LANCE:** TDR-5S Thunderbolt; GLT-4L Guillotine; BLR-1G Battlemaster; ON1-M Orion

**FIRE LANCE:** SHD-2H Shadow Hawk; GRF-3M Griffin; CRD-3R Crusader; GRF-1N Griffin

**STRIKER LANCE:** PXH-1 Phoenix Hawk; COM-2D Commando; WLF-1 Wolfhound; BSW-X1 Bushwacker
